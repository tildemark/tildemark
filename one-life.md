## 📌 PROJECT NOTES & ENGINEERING CLAUSES

### 1. Warranty & Service Deliverables

* **Hardware Warranty:** All core Dahua components (NVR, Cameras, PoE Switch) carry a **One (1) Year Local Warranty** covering factory defects. Any components found defective under normal operating conditions will be replaced or repaired at no charge.
* **Exclusions:** The warranty strictly excludes hardware damage resulting from incoming power surges, lightning strikes, localized building flooding, or unauthorized client tampering/reconfiguration.
* **Service Aftercare:** Includes **30 days of complimentary on-site technical support** starting from the official date of project turnover. Any service calls or physical site adjustments requested beyond this 30-day window will incur a standard technician mobilization fee.

### 2. Implementation & Aesthetic Standards

* **Conduit Integrity:** To ensure maximum wire life and minimize signal drag over the upgraded CAT6 infrastructure, structural routing will prioritize **continuous, cold-bent swept profiles using a professional internal bending spring** rather than rigid segmented joints. This limits cable snags and prevents moisture pooling points.
* **Enclosure Security:** The centralized head-end hardware (NVR, PoE Switch, and UPS) will be securely enclosed within a lockable, wall-mounted 6U server cabinet located in the designated **"DVR/MONITOR"** room to restrict physical data access to authorized personnel only.

### 3. Client Prerequisites & Infrastructure Responsibilities

* **Power Provisioning:** The client shall ensure a dedicated, active 220V AC electrical convenience outlet is installed adjacent to the specified server cabinet mounting location prior to technical mobilization.
* **Network Integration:** To enable remote mobile camera viewing on smartphones or laptops via the cloud framework, the client must provision an active internet connection (router/modem with an open LAN gateway port) within the DVR/Monitor room.
* **Structural Access:** The client shall grant full, unhindered access to all interior rooms, corridors, and ceiling spaces during the installation window, and assist in securing necessary internal building or management clearances.

### 4. Commercial Validity

* This cost estimate remains firm and valid for a period of **thirty (30) days** from the date of submission. Any modifications to the 16-camera layout drawn on the architectural blueprint will require a mutually signed Change Order before work commences.
