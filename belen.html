Turning Waste into Wonders: Creativity Meets Cleanliness

Standing beneath the banner of Gloria in Excelsis Deo, this Belen represents our community's resourcefulness. We transformed common household waste into a sacred scene, proving that beauty can be found in sustainability.

From Trash to Treasure

  • Egg Trays: Repurposed to create the textured "stone" backdrop and main signage.
  • Eco Bags: Styled to create the vibrant robes of the Holy Family.
  • Coconut Husks & Leaves: Fallen nature gathered to thatch the roof.
  • Cut Wood Branches: Scavenged wood used for the rustic pillars.
  • Live Plants: Integrated to symbolize life, growth, and cleanliness.

"Just as the manger was a humble beginning, these recycled materials remind us that great things often start from the simplest parts."
